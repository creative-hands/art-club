About

Patient guidance with room for creative confidence.

Creative Hands Art Club is designed for families and adults who want structured, friendly creative learning without a competitive classroom feel.

Approach

Clear instruction, flexible expression.

The instructor can adapt each class for absolute beginners, returning learners, and students who want more challenge. The goal is steady skill-building, comfort, and creative joy across art forms.

Club values

Welcoming: beginner-friendly for kids and adults.

Creative: drawing, painting, crafts, movement, and workshops.

Practical: age-wise batches with clear schedules.
